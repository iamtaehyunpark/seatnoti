# SeatNoti

## Project Description

SeatNoti is a Chrome extension designed to help students and professionals keep track of available seating in lectures, meetings, or other events. With real-time notifications about free seats, users can maximize their productivity and never miss a chance to participate in activities that matter.

### Key Features:
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Get instant alerts when seats become available so you can quickly grab one before they fill up.
- **Schedule Exporting:** Seamlessly export your class or meeting schedules to your preferred calendar (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.) to keep everything organized in one place.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Easy to navigate and operate, allowing users of all tech levels to utilize its powerful features.
- **Customizable Settings:** Tailor the notification preferences and export options according to your specific needs.

### Installation:
1. Download the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
2. Click on the extension icon in the toolbar to set it up.
3. Configure your schedule and notification settings.

### Usage:
- Open the extension and input your class/meeting schedule.
- Enable notifications to receive updates about free seats.
- Check your calendar regularly to view exported schedules.
